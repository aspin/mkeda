Mission

MKeda is the regional distributor of global manufacturer Keda Company, serving customers in the U.S., Canada, Mexico, and South America. Keda Company produces various bearings for different gasoline and diesel engine models. Keda Company has state-of-art facilities in two locations in China (Wenzhou and Anhui) with a combined occupancy area of 25,000 square meters (or 269,000 square feet) and production capacity of 45 million pieces of engine bearings every year. Keda products have been sold to customers in Asia, Europe, Africa, North America, and South America. Together, we ensure that our products are manufactured with the highest quality and guarantee that customer satisfaction is our first priority.
